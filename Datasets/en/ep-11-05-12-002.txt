<CHAPTER ID="002">
Statement by the President
<SPEAKER ID="006" NAME="President">
Given the tragedy that has struck the region of Murcia and specifically, the town of Lorca, I should like to express, on behalf of the Presidency, our solidarity with that community and with all the victims, several of whom have been killed. The President will convey, on behalf of Parliament, a message of solidarity and condolence to the victims and to the community affected.
