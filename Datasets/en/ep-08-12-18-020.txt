Adjournment of the session
<SPEAKER ID="333" NAME="President">
The Minutes of this sitting shall be submitted to Parliament for approval at the start of the next part-session. If there are no comments, I shall immediately send the approved resolutions to their recipients.
<P>
I declare the session of the European Parliament adjourned.
